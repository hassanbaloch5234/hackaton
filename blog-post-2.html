Green Kayak

GreenKayak is an environmental organization that allows individuals to make a direct impact on the cleanliness of their local water bodies. This organization, started in Copenhagen in 2017 and now available in Stockholm, Sweden, provides free kayak rentals to people willing to pick up litter from the water during their trip. In this way, GreenKayak engages the local community in environmental activism, transforming the act of kayaking into a sustainable, eco-friendly practice. In 2020, Green Kayak became a UNESCO Green Citizen project. A total of 17868 kg of trash have been collected that year only with the help of 12047 volunteers.

Their unique initiative not only promotes physical activity and outdoor recreation but also instills a deep sense of responsibility for the environment among participants. The collected litter is documented and categorized, providing valuable data about pollution levels and types, which can further inform future environmental protection efforts. By combining leisure with an opportunity to care for the environment, Green Kayak provides a unique model for community-led environmental stewardship. Come book a trip and help keeping the waters clean: https://www.greenkayak.org/
